I

The Syro Malankara Church

The Syro Malankara Catholic Church traces its origins to the ancient St Thomas Christian tradition of Kerala, India. Founded in 1930 by Archbishop Mar Ivanios, who led a historic reunion with Rome, our Church preserves the West Syriac liturgical heritage while remaining in full communion with the Holy See.

52 AD
St Thomas the Apostle brings the faith to Kerala
1930
Archbishop Mar Ivanios leads the reunion with Rome, founding the Syro Malankara Catholic Church
Present
A vibrant Church spanning continents, rooted in the West Syriac Antiochian liturgical tradition
II

Our Singapore Mission

St Mary's Syro Malankara Catholic Mission, Singapore, serves the faithful of the Malankara rite in the Lion City. Our mission brings together the Malayali Catholic diaspora and all who are drawn to the rich, contemplative beauty of the Syro Malankara liturgy — a living bridge between apostolic heritage and the people of Singapore.

We gather weekly to celebrate the Holy Qurbana — the ancient Eucharistic liturgy — in both Malayalam and English, nurturing a community of prayer, fellowship, and service.
